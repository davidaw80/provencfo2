Reasons to love ProvenCFO.

For most law firms, accounting means stress, worry, frustration, wasted time, and doesn't provide much real value. However, having a system which works, will result in confidence and financial insight, which will help your business in a big way.

System Creation

We ensure you have the right system in place to ensure your accounting is smooth and insighful. We implement systems like Clio in conjuction with Xero to get you the most out of your data.

Proven Processes

Having good systems is only part of the puzzle. We implement recurring processes to marry with great systems to ensure you are always operating in the now, and not months in the past.

Relevant Reporting

Reports shouldn't only be useful for tax and compliance reasons. Your information needs to be relevant, reliable, and real-time. Your accounting also needs to provide real-time business intelligence.

Forward Looking

Every firm has different goals. Some may want to grow while others may want to simply take more moneyhome or be more profitable. No matter the goal the path to get there is found in the numbers.

Cash Flow-Focused

Cash is always king since your business requires flow to keep things running and your employees paid. Key metrics, growth predictions, and monitoring change always needs to tie back to cash.

Feedback & Optimization

The best law firms have a regular cycle of forecasting, tracking, and adjusting as change occurs. Having the right system is like having an amazing road-map of your goals and real-time accountability on how that plan is playing out.
